ABOUT

EPICENTRE:

  a focal point, as of activity; center

To us, Berlin is a central point of lifestyle and arts in our time.

Our goal is to do quality videos for people,
events and entreprises which fit to the Berlin state of mind.

We do event videos and commercials for projects we trust with an esthetic point of view and a an homemade know-how.

EVENT VIDEOS

We shoot and edit for events to make them a bit less ephemeral.

Berlin is certainly one of the cities in Europe with the most amazing and original events.

Epicentre has the willing to do events videos in a modern and sharp way. We want to give Berliners, videos as originals and daring as the events the city offers us.

COMMERCIAL VIDEOS

Berlin is full of young creators, amazing brands and entrepreneurs full of ideas.

We want to help these promising brands with esthetic videos which look like them.

Our ambition is to give these brands the visual promotion they deserve.

We work with our clients and take care of everything, we organize all the shooting from the pre-production to the final product.
