ABOUT

EPICENTRE:

  noun /ˈep.ɪ.sen.tər/ The absolute center of something

EPICENTRE is a Berlin based production company, specialized in commercials and event videos.

Our ambition is to make videos which share our vision: fresh, keen, accurate and aesthetic.

In our opinion, Berlin is the central point for lifestyle and arts in our time; it’s the city of the present, but also the future.

As filmmakers, it makes sense that we follow this state of mind by creating quality videos with a homemade 'know-how' and an artful point of view.

EVENT VIDEOS

Berlin is certainly one of the cities with the most amazing and original events in Europe.

We have the desire to make event videos in a modern and sharp way. We want to give Berliners videos as original, and as daring, as the events this city provides.

Event videos are the best way to make these occasions last, and at the same time promote the next ones.

We personally shoot and take care of all the post-production work. Our ambition is to make these events a little less fleeting.

COMMERCIALS

Berlin is full of young creators; teeming with entrepreneurial ideas and amazing brands.

We want to give these brands a video that fits them: independent, original, free-minded, authentic and contemporary.

Our way of working is flexible. We can adapt your defined ideas into a faithful and professional content. We can also work closely with you to create the concept together: searching for the ideas and establishing the script. Each time to achieve the same goal: to promote your work in an inventive and sophisticated way.
In every project we take care of the entire production process: casting, shooting, editing and all the post-production.

Our goal is to give you the visual promotion you deserve!.
